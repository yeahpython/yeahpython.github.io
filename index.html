Chrome extensions
I enjoy making Chrome extensions in my spare time. Here are some chrome extensions I've made:
  • Emoji pets : Generate a bunch of animated emojis that wander around your page! At time of writing this has more than 6000 users.
  • Filter Anything Anywhere : Scans pages for things that looks like feeds and filters them with a single blacklist.
  • Journey New Tab : A new tab page with a horizontal timeline.
  • One-Click Controls for Google Play Music : Control playback from any tab.
